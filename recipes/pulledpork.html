Pulled pork

Prep time: 30 minutes

cooking time: 4,5 hours

Nr. of servings: depends on pork size but a lot :)

Ingredients:
  • 1,2 kg of pork thigh or shoulder
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons grinded red paprika
  • 1 teaspoon grinded black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of grinded cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of grinded roman cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried marjoram
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 30 g of salt (himalayan preferred)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato pure
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil and a little for the meat frying
  • 4 tablespoons of water
  • 1 onion
  • 150 nl water
  • 100 ml ketchup (125 g)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of ACV or wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of worcestershire sauce

Directions

How to, here goes

Mix the dry ingredients all together in a bowl. Add the tomato pure and the 4 tablespoons of oil
and add the 4 tablespoons of water. Now mix these all together for a perfect mass mixture
Massage nicely in the meat
At this stage I'd recommend to start heating up the oven to 130 celsius
Heat up a big enough sauce pan (no plastic parts since it will go in the oven) with a lid.
Now add the oil and the meat and fry all sides
Chop up and add the onion and the rest of the mass, the ketchup, water, ACV and the worcestershire and soy sauce
Mix well and once it start to boil cover and put it in the preheated oven for 4 hours

Once finished take it out of the oven BE EXTRA CAREFUL BECAUSE PAN IS HOT and take the meat out into a bowl
Use 2 forks or similar to pull the meat apart but don't make it into tiny bits but chunks.
If you like it more moist you can add the sauce from the pan. Now you can also boil the sauce a bit more and use it as a topping.

Some more options for fun

For a superb sandwich use some hamburger buns or any type of your choice, and add some cheese mayonnaise and some pickles for a perfect sandwich experience

ENJOY!!! :)
