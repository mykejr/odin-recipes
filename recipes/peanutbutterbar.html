Peanut butter bars

This recipe can also be Keto friendly, simple to prepare

Prep time: 5 minutes

cooking time: zero, they need cooling

Nr. of servings: depends on how much calories you need :)

Ingredients:

Cream

  • 250 grams of peanut butter
  • 100-120 grams of almond flower
  • 50 g sweetener (erytrol)

Topping

  • 120 g chocolate
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Directions

Step 1 cream

Mix the peanut butter with the sweetener and the almond flower in a bowl using a spatula
once it is an even mixture put it in cake shaped square or round holder
might be a good idea to use foil so wont stick to the sides and spread the mixture evenly and push it down a little

Step 2 topping

melt the chocolate in another bowl and mix with coconut oil evenly
Pour it on top of the mixture and spread evenly.
cover with foil and put it in the freezer for 2-3 hours
take out from freezer and the cake bowl, this is where it is handy to have one that can be opened from alll sides
simply cut with a knife into preferred shape and size
