Basic muffin recipe

Prep time: 20 minutes

cooking time: 25 minutes

Nr. of servings: 20 cups

Ingredients:
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 125 ml vegetable oil
  • 250 ml semi-skimmed-milk
  • 400 g flower
  • 3 tsp backing powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions

Step 1

Heat oven to 200 celsius
in a bowl beat up eggs lightly

Step 2

add vegetable oil and 250 ml milk and beat till combined
Sadd 250 g sugar and whisk till the batter is smooth

Step 3

stiff in the flower and backing powder and mix untill just smooth (don't overmix)
fill the muffins into muffin tray and into the oven
